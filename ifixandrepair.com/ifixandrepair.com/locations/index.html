After that, the price is not subject to change.

Guidelines and limitations: The price match is available for parts and repairs we have in stock. The price match is not available for special order repairs. Only one repair per customer and per day is allowed. Price match guarantee does not apply to: Repair prices that are a result of a special discount, mall-in offers, offers that include financing, bundle offers or auctions. Prices from any repair provider that doesn't have a brick and mortar store. Repair prices that require a minimum amount of purchases.
